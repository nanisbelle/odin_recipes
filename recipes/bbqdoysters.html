BBQ'd Oysters

When consuming Oysters it is extremely important to grill them fresh and clean. Purchasing large Oyster are perferred.

Once the oysters have been brushed clean

  • Heat the grill to 400 degrees
  • Grill oysters over direct heat.

Lets Get Grillin'

  1. Place Oysters flat shell up and directly over the flames.
  2. Cover the Barbecue grill
  3. Let them cook for 10 minutes or until the shells pop open naturally.

Shuck Oysters & Enjoy!

How to Shuck Oysters?
  • Place the shucking knife between the slightly agate flute part of the shell
  • Slip the knife and twist all in one swoop till the shell pops open.

Best Served:

  • Freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Hot sauce (of your choice)
  • Pinch of salt
