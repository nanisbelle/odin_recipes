Shrimp & Grits

My husbands favorite shrimps & grits! Try it out! Let us know what you think!

Ingredients:

  • Andouille Sausage (Fully Cooked) - 12 Ounce
  • Uncooked Shrimp (Peeled & Deveined) - 12 Ounce
  • Boiling Water - 3 Cups
  • Instant Cheesy Grits - 1 Box(12 Ounce Packets)
  • Butter - 1 Tablespoon
  • Chedder Cheese - 1 cup

Seasoning Ingredients:

  • Cajun Seasoning - 1 Teaspoon
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Parsley - 1 Teaspoon

Directions

  1. In a skillet, cook the andouille sausage over medium high heat until hot.
  2. Remove from the skillet and slice the andouille sausage into perfect little coins. Set Aside.
  3. In a pot add the water stir together the grits and boiling water.
  4. Add Butter & Cheddar Cheese
  5. Let sit till thickened
  6. Add the peeled shrimp into the skillet. Sprinkle with the seasoning listed above.
  7. Stir around and cook for 2-3 minutes or until pink and curled.
  8. Spoon grits into serving bowl, Top with sausage and cooked shrimp.

Enjoy!
