Seafood Crab Salad

The Best Seafood Crab Pasta Salad! Try this Summer Delight! Best for summer parties or when you are in the mod for some fresh cold deliciousness in your life!

Ingredients:

  • Elbow Pasta - 1 pound
  • Imitaion Crab - 14 ounces
  • Canned Peas - 1/2 Cup
  • Red Onion(Peeled & Chopped) - 1/2 Cup
  • Celery(Chopped) - 1/2 Cup
  • Red Bell Pepper(Seeded & Chopped) - 1/2 Cup
  • Mayonnaise - 3/4 Cup

Ingredients:

  • Fresh Dill(Chopped) - 1 Tablespoon
  • Fresh Chives (Chopped) - Garnish
  • Salt & Pepper as Desired.

Directions

  1. Boil the Elbow Pasta, gerneously salt the water. Cook the pasta.Drain pasta and run under cold water to stop the cooking.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, add the cooked pasta, crab, peas, onion, celery, bell pepper, mayo, and dill.
  3. Gently stir to combine until everything is well coated.
  4. Add the Seasonings
  5. Cover & Store in fridge for 1 hr.

Best Served:

  • Served Cold
  • Perferred Summer Delight
  • With Seafood Lovers!
