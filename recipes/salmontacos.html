Salmon Tacos

My favorite go to Salmon Tacos! Try these incredible life changing Salmon Tacos you never knew you've been missing in your life!

Ingredients:

  • Salmon Fillet
  • Tortillas
  • 2 Cups of Shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup of chopped cilantro
  • sour cream
  • fresh salsa
  • Wedge of Lime

Seasoning:

(For the Salmon)

  • Chili Powder
  • Seafood Bullion
  • Paprika
  • Butter
  • Basil

Lets Bake This Salmon

  1. Preheat Oven to 400f
  2. Clean Salmon Fillet
  3. Season salmon using:seasoning ingredients above.
  4. Place seasoned salmon on cookie sheet topped with foil.
  5. Bake 15-20 minutes

Taco Prep:

Warm Tortillas add the following:

  1. Baked Salmon
  2. Shredded Cabbage
  3. Sour Cream
  4. Fresh Salsa
  5. Sprits of Lime
  6. Topped of with Fresh Cilantro

Best Served:

  • Warm
  • With your favorite beverage
  • With great company!
